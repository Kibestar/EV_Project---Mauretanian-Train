The whole project can be grouped into two main parts where future developments could make a difference: The first beeing the charging islands and the second the train itself.

\subsection{Impact on the Charing Islands}
As of now is the charging islands are designed to produce energy from solar for the train only. Since the islands are stationed at the train stations in vilages, the project could be expanded to provide energy for the whole village. This could make a big impact since many rural areas still rely on fossil fuels for generators and heating. This would go hand in hand with a more and more reported increase in the deployment of home energy storage systems \cite{ElectrekArticle2024}. This would allow the villagers to store energy in case of an overproduction and use it when needed. 

On big change that would masively incease the efficiency of the storrage would be the development of batteries that are more suitable for the wide temperature range of the desert. This would reduce the auxiliary energy consumption for the cooling and heating of the batteries and increase the overall efficiency of the system.

\subsection{Impact on the Train}
As for the train itself, the same thing applies for the batteries. If the batteries are more efficient and can operate in a wider temperature range, the train would be more efficient and require less energy for cooling and heating.

Another thing, which is also reported as an upcoming development, is the increase in the efficiency of power converters \cite{ChargedEVsArticle}. Since the train incorporates a lot of power electronics for the traction system, this would have a big impact on the overall efficiency of the train.
