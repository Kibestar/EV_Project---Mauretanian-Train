\subsection{Overall Architecture}
A complete overview of the power transfer can be seen in figure .... For the current and power calculations, the maximum power is considered, as discussed in section ....
\newline During standstill and acceleration, power is drawn from the 25 kV 1-phase AC overhead line. This 25 kV AC power is converted into 950 V AC before going onboard. This is done because the lower voltage is suitable for the traction equipment. Besides, 950 V AC is below the IEEE definition of high-voltage (1 kV AC) \cite{HV_IEEE}, saving difficulties around handling arc-flash risk, insulation coordination and creepage distances.
